Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about roof rejuvenation and what to expect from Roof Fortress.

Most asphalt shingle roofs between 5 and 20 years old are excellent candidates. During our free inspection, we assess granule loss, shingle flexibility, and overall structural integrity. We'll give you an honest assessment — if your roof genuinely needs replacement, we'll tell you.
Our treatments come with a 6-year warranty and are proven to add at least 6 years of life per application. With up to four treatments possible over the life of your roof, that's up to 24 years of total added protection. Many customers treat their roof multiple times before ever needing a replacement.
The rejuvenation treatment is designed to restore your shingles' natural appearance — deepening their color and restoring uniformity. Many customers report their roof looks years newer after treatment. The product is clear and does not create a visible coating or change the texture.
Unlike surface coatings or sealants, our GreenSoy™ Technology — developed with molecular experts at Iowa State University — penetrates deep into the asphalt shingle structure. It replenishes the bio-based oils asphalt loses over time, restoring up to 50% of structural flexibility and dramatically improving wind and hail resistance. It addresses the root cause of aging, not just the surface symptoms.
Our treatment does not void existing manufacturer warranties in most cases, and many insurance companies view professional rejuvenation as a proactive maintenance measure. We provide documentation of our work for your records. We recommend checking your specific policy, and we're happy to provide supporting materials to your insurer if needed.
Our crew arrives in the morning, does a final safety walkthrough, and applies the treatment using low-pressure application equipment. The process is quiet and non-disruptive. You don't need to be home. The treatment cures within a few hours and is rain-safe within 24 hours. We clean up completely before we leave and email you a post-service report.
