The Science

What Is GreenSoy™ Rejuvenation?

GreenSoy™ is a patented bio-based rejuvenation formula developed in partnership with Iowa State University. It's derived from soybean oil — the same natural oils that keep asphalt shingles flexible and waterproof when they were new.

Over time, UV exposure and weather cycles cause those oils to evaporate, leaving shingles brittle, cracked, and vulnerable. GreenSoy™ penetrates the shingle surface and replenishes those lost oils — restoring the shingle to near-original condition without any tear-off.

The result: a roof that flexes instead of cracks, sheds water instead of absorbing it, and outlasts its expected lifespan by years.
