# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system, reflecting the current theme configuration.

## 1. Color Palette

Our system employs a distinct color palette designed to provide a modern and cohesive visual experience.

-   **Color Mode**: This theme is configured for a `light` environment, ensuring optimal contrast and readability for day-time or well-lit interfaces.
-   **Primary Color**: `#4DBFBF` - A vibrant and commanding color, used for key interactive elements, primary calls to action, and brand emphasis.
-   **Secondary Color**: `#0D1B2A` - A deep, rich secondary color, providing strong contrast and depth for supporting elements, less prominent UI components, and background accents.
-   **Tertiary Color**: `#8B5E3C` - An earthy accent color, adding warmth and sophistication. It is used sparingly for highlights, badges, or decorative details.
-   **Neutral Color**: `#1A1A1A` - A dark, almost black, neutral base. While the theme is light mode, this deep neutral serves as a foundational color for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, providing robust contrast against lighter text where appropriate.

## 2. Typography

Our typography system is crafted for clarity, readability, and brand expression across all user interfaces.

-   **Headline Font**: `manrope` - Chosen for its modern, geometric characteristics, providing a clean and impactful presence for all headlines and titles.
-   **Body Font**: `inter` - A highly versatile and legible sans-serif typeface, ideal for all body text, ensuring comfortable reading across various screen sizes.
-   **Label Font**: `inter` - Consistent with the body font, `inter` is also used for labels, ensuring uniformity and clarity for form fields, buttons, and UI annotations.

## 3. Shape and Form

The geometric properties of our UI elements contribute to the overall feel of the system.

-   **Roundedness**: `1` (Subtle roundedness) - Elements feature a subtle degree of roundedness, offering a soft, approachable aesthetic without compromising on a clean, modern edge. This provides a gentle visual break from sharp corners.

## 4. Spacing

Our spacing system ensures visual hierarchy, readability, and a balanced layout across all components.

-   **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - A balanced approach to whitespace is adopted, providing a comfortable and standard amount of space within and between UI elements. This promotes good readability and visual organization without being overly dense or excessively spacious.
